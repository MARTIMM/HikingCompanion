Hiking Companion

The Hiking Companion is your guide on your trips no matter if they are long or short, city trips or hikes. At first, there are no hikes available but you can record your own trips with this application. However, there are trips available on the playstore which can be installed. Currently the following are available;

  • SufiTrail: An ancient trail from Istanbul to Konya
  • SultansTrail: A hike from Vienna to Istanbul
  • HaarlemTrips: City trips in and around Haarlem North-Holland, Netherlands

The program is easy to work with. A few buttons on top of every page and a menu to select more pages.
